Privacy Policy – Smart Unit Converter

Last updated: June 1, 2026

This Privacy Policy applies to the Smart Unit Converter app (the "Application") for mobile devices, developed by Yousef Alkhamesi (the "Service Provider").

The Application is a fully offline utility app that does not collect, transmit, or share any personal data. It does not require internet access to function.

The only data stored is your theme preference (dark/light mode), saved locally on your device using SharedPreferences. This data never leaves your device.
